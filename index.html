History

De Cairo’s Mountain and Ocean View Resort traces its beginnings to December 9, 2023, when it was developed as a serene retreat overlooking both the mountains and the coastline of Boljoon, Cebu. Originally built as a modest rest house for family and friends by Rene Cairo Jr. also known as Junjun Cairo, the owner of the resort, it gradually evolved into a full resort as more visitors began to appreciate its peaceful setting and scenic views.

Life of the Owner

Rene Cairo Jr. also known as Junjun Cairo by many people, owner of The De Cairo’s Resort, was born on December 9, 1983, in Ylaya, Upper Becerril, Boljoon, Cebu. Now 42 years old and the sixth child in his family, he later moved to Manila, where he met his wife, Margie Cairo. While living in the city, Rene handled several business ventures, including an alcohol business, before expanding into the hospitality industry with De Cairo’s Resort. He and Margie are blessed with three children—two boys and one girl.

The success story of the business

The success of De Cairo’s Resort comes from the experience and good business skills of its owner, Rene Cairo Jr. Before opening the resort, he managed different businesses, which helped him learn important skills. Because of this, he was able to develop De Cairo’s Resort into a growing and competitive business. With continuous improvements,and the support of his manager, the resort continues to grow and keep its customers satisfied

The challenges met by the owner

The founder faced several challenges when starting the business, such as limited finances, difficulties in completing requirements, and a lack of resources. Although they had three loyal employees, managing daily operations was still tough, especially when supplies or food were not always enough. Even with these obstacles, the business continued to operate and serve many customers, steadily improving over time.

The business 10 years after

“Ten years from now, De Cairos Mountain and Ocean View Resort envisions itself as a well-established and trusted destination known for its peaceful ambiance, quality service, and strong connection to nature. We aim to continuously improve our facilities while maintaining sustainability and environmental responsibility. Our long-term goal is to expand our services, create more local employment opportunities, and be recognized as one of the preferred mountain and ocean view resorts in the region—where guests can truly relax, reconnect, and create lasting memories.”
